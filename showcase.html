“Shot in Texas” Film Showcase

Giant (1956)

"A sprawling saga of family, power, and oil."

Director: George Stevens

Stars: Elizabeth Taylor, Rock Hudson, James Dean

Synopsis: A Texas ranching dynasty wrestles with shifting power, prejudice, and pride across generations.

Filming Location: Marfa, TX

Fun Fact: The set hotel still stands in Marfa and is a major pilgrimage site for fans.

No Country for Old Men (2007)

"There are no clean getaways."

Director: Joel & Ethan Coen

Stars: Javier Bardem, Josh Brolin, Tommy Lee Jones

Synopsis: A hunter finds a briefcase full of cash near the Rio Grande, drawing the attention of a relentless killer.

Filming Location: Eagle Pass, Sanderson, Marfa, TX

Fun Fact: Many street scenes were filmed in Dallas and the surrounding area.

Dallas Buyers Club (2013)

"Dare to live."

Director: Jean-Marc Vallée

Stars: Matthew McConaughey, Jared Leto, Jennifer Garner

Synopsis: A Texas man with HIV fights for access to experimental medication in the 1980s.

Filming Location: New Orleans & Dallas-area sets

Fun Fact: McConaughey lost nearly 50 pounds and won an Oscar for his performance.

Boyhood (2014)

"12 years. A moment at a time."

Director: Richard Linklater

Stars: Ellar Coltrane, Ethan Hawke, Patricia Arquette

Synopsis: Filmed over 12 years with the same actors, this coming-of-age tale follows a boy growing up in Texas.

Filming Location: Houston, Austin, San Marcos, Dallas

Fun Fact: Linklater shot in short yearly sessions between 2001–2013.
