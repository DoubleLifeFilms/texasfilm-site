“Shot in Texas” Film Showcase

Giant (1956)

“The legendary Texas oil epic.”

Director: George Stevens

Stars: Elizabeth Taylor, James Dean, Rock Hudson

Location: Marfa, TX

Fun Fact: James Dean died shortly after finishing this role.

Synopsis: A sprawling family drama about wealth, race, and land in West Texas.

Friday Night Lights (2004)

“Hope comes alive under the lights.”

Director: Peter Berg

Stars: Billy Bob Thornton, Derek Luke

Location: Odessa & Austin, TX

Fun Fact: Based on the nonfiction book by H.G. Bissinger.

Synopsis: A high school football team carries the hopes of a small Texas town.

No Country for Old Men (2007)

“There are no clean getaways.”

Directors: Joel & Ethan Coen

Stars: Javier Bardem, Josh Brolin, Tommy Lee Jones

Location: Marfa, Sanderson, Eagle Pass, TX

Fun Fact: Won 4 Oscars including Best Picture.

Synopsis: A hunter stumbles upon drug money — and becomes the hunted.
