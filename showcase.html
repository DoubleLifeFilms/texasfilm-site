“Shot in Texas” Film Showcase

Giant (1956)

Director: George Stevens

Stars: Elizabeth Taylor, James Dean, Rock Hudson

Filming Location: Marfa, TX

Fun Fact: The cast and crew lived in Marfa for weeks; today it's a pilgrimage site for cinephiles.

Friday Night Lights (2004)

Director: Peter Berg

Stars: Billy Bob Thornton, Derek Luke

Filming Location: Odessa, TX

Fun Fact: Inspired the hit TV series — both filmed entirely in Texas.

No Country for Old Men (2007)

Directors: Joel & Ethan Coen

Stars: Javier Bardem, Josh Brolin, Tommy Lee Jones

Filming Location: West Texas (Sanderson, Eagle Pass)

Fun Fact: The Coens used real towns and motels — the stark landscape became a character itself.

Dallas Buyers Club (2013)

Director: Jean-Marc Vallée

Stars: Matthew McConaughey, Jared Leto

Filming Location: New Orleans (but set in Dallas)

Fun Fact: Though not shot in Dallas, the film is based on the real-life Dallasite Ron Woodroof.

RoboCop (2014)

Director: José Padilha

Stars: Joel Kinnaman, Gary Oldman

Filming Location: Dallas, TX

Fun Fact: Parts of downtown Dallas were used as a stand-in for futuristic Detroit.

JFK (1991)

Director: Oliver Stone

Stars: Kevin Costner, Tommy Lee Jones

Filming Location: Dallas & Dealey Plaza

Fun Fact: Dealey Plaza was locked down for days to recreate the JFK assassination.
