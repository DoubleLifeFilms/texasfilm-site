“Shot in Texas” Film Showcase

Giant (1956)

Director: George Stevens

Stars: James Dean, Elizabeth Taylor, Rock Hudson

Filmed in: Marfa, TX

Fun Fact: James Dean died shortly after filming his scenes.

Friday Night Lights (2004)

Director: Peter Berg

Stars: Billy Bob Thornton, Garrett Hedlund, Derek Luke

Filmed in: Odessa & Austin, TX

Fun Fact: Based on the 1990 nonfiction book by H.G. Bissinger.

No Country for Old Men (2007)

Directors: Joel & Ethan Coen

Stars: Javier Bardem, Josh Brolin, Tommy Lee Jones

Filmed in: Marfa, Sanderson, Eagle Pass, TX

Fun Fact: Won 4 Oscars including Best Picture.

Boys Don't Cry (1999)

Director: Kimberly Peirce

Stars: Hilary Swank, Chloë Sevigny, Peter Sarsgaard

Filmed in: Greenville & Dallas, TX

Fun Fact: Hilary Swank won an Oscar for Best Actress.

RoboCop (1987)

Director: Paul Verhoeven

Stars: Peter Weller, Nancy Allen, Ronny Cox

Filmed in: Dallas, TX (as Detroit)

Fun Fact: Dallas City Hall doubled as OCP headquarters.

Bonnie and Clyde (1967)

Director: Arthur Penn

Stars: Warren Beatty, Faye Dunaway

Filmed in: Dallas, TX and surrounding areas

Fun Fact: Sparked the "New Hollywood" film movement.
